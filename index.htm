What is AI?

Artificial intelligence is the simulation of human intelligence processes by machines, especially computer systems. Specific applications of AI include expert systems, natural language processing, speech recognition and machine vision.

How does AI work?

AI learns from tons of data and examples. It looks for patterns in the data to make predictions or decisions. It can do things like recognize faces in photos, understand speech, or recommend movies you might like. It's all about teaching computers to learn and make decisions like humans, but much faster and with a lot of data.


Think of AI as a really smart friend who learns from examples. It's like teaching a child how to recognize a cat. You show them lots of pictures of cats and say, "This is a cat." After seeing many examples, they start to understand what makes a cat a cat, like pointy ears and whiskers.
